About me

Hi, I am Arkadiy. Currently I work as a research fellow in the University of Oxford at Andrew Goodwin group.

My research is focused in understanding the structure and function of disordered single crystals. I am developing a novel method for analysing diffuse scattering from single crystals called the three dimensional difference pair distribution function (3D-ΔPDF).

Selected publications

Disorder by design

In addition to a catalogue of strongly correlated disordered procrystalline meterials done by Alistar, the paper presents the calculation of the phonon dispersion curves in two dimensional oxynitride disordered and procrystal realisations. It is shown that by controlling the local order it is possible to broaden specific dispersion bands. In the current example, the lowest acoustic bands are selectively distorted. In real materials such techniques can be used to produce better thermoelectric meterials. [...]

Local order in thermoelectric Ge4Bi2Te7

Ge4Bi2Te7 is a perspective thermoelectric, whose low thermal conductivity is seemingly incompatible with the simple NaCl type average structure of the material. In this work we show that the real structure of the crystal is complicated and consists of NaCl-type slabs separated by planar Van der Waals gaps. [...]

Disorder in Al-Cu-Ta complex metallic compound

The paper presents the structure of a novel hexagonal compound hP386-Al57.4Cu3.6Ta39.0. In addition to the complex average structure the crystal shows disorder in a form of rare stacking faults. Using diffuse scattering and the difference electron density map we were able to model the structure of such stacking faults and the metallic clusters they produce. [...]

Accuracy of 3D-ΔPDF

The following paper carefully assesses the accuracy of the 3D-ΔPDF refinement method. We show that the method provides excellent accuracy even for very weak correlations at large interatomic vectors. In the current case we found that the orientation of two 1,3,5-tris(2,2-dimethylpropionylamino)benzene molecules separated by 100Å are not totaly independent. They were found to have the same orientaion in 49.76(4)% of the cases. [...]

Yell software

This paper presents Yell, a program for 3D-ΔPDF modelling. The program allows to refine all types of short range order correlations, allows to apply constraints of arbitrary algebraic complexity, and performs diffuse scattering calculation using fast FFT based algorithm.

Basic concepts of 3D-ΔPDF

This paper provides an introduction into the Three Dimensional Pair Distribution Function (3D-ΔPDF) method.

Software

Meerkat

A software for reconstructing reciprocal space of the single crystal. The reconstruction is similar to the unwrap function of Crysalis program or similar software, except Meerkat reconstruct the whole three dimensional volume instead of planes. It is a descendent of Xcavate software, and contains a number of improvements. Firstly it allows to make reconstructions in crystallographic coordinates, secondly, due to the optimised algorithms the reconstructions work much faster.

The program is distributed as a python library. in order to install it you will require a distribution of python (I recommend Anaconda)

pip install meerkat
